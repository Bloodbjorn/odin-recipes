Beef Stew

Description

Made with good cubed steak meat and chopped veggies beef stew is one of those highly versitile dishes that you can slow cook or make within an hour but generally the slower it's cooked the better the flavor will be by the time it's dinnertime. This recipe requires a large stew pot, a frying pan to cook the onions and meat in and a pan to cook the stew bone with meat.

Ingredients

  • 4 8-ounce serloin steaks diced into cubes
  • 8 medium sized russet potatos
  • 6 medium sized carrots
  • 1 medium onion
  • 1 stew bone with meat(this bone should have bone marrow inside)
  • 2 cans of beef stock
  • 2 cans of water
  • 4 beef bullion cubes
  • Sea Salt
  • Black Pepper

Directions

  1. Turn on oven to 350 degrees and place the stew bone with meat into a pan and salt and pepper on all surfaces of exposed marrow
  2. Place the stew bone with meat into the oven for 10-15 minutes or until bone marrow is melted and bubbling
  3. Get out a large stew pot and place on the largest burner on the stove
  4. peel and dice the russet potatos and carrots
  5. When the stew bone with meat and somewhat cooled add to stew pot, deglaze the pan with a can of beef stock and scrape and pour and anything left into the stew pot, put on medium heat
  6. open and pour the remaining can of beef stock, and both cans of water into the stew pot adding in 4 beef bullion cubes
  7. Add the diced carrots and potatos and place stew lid on and allow to slow cook on medium low or leave on medium to cook
  8. When the potatos and carrots begin to become soft dice the steaks into cubes and cook on medium-high heat on a frying pan until each side is seered and add them to the stew pot
  9. Dice the medium onion and add to the same frying pan, sauteing until soft and add to the stew pot
  10. Allow the stew to cook another 5-10 minutes or until the meat is done to its desired amount
  11. Remove the stew bone and serve
