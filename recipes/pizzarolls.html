Pizza Rolls

Description

You don't have to wait an hour to have the taste of hot home made pepperoni bread. it's as close as your fridge and as quick as under 30 minutes including prep time. Hearty. tasty and satsifying. Your family will love this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 Package Grands Style Biscuits
  • about 4 oz pepperoni slices
  • about 4 oz shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions

  1. With a rolling pin or hands, flatten each biscuit to about the size of a small flour tortilla
  2. Place about 1 to 1 1/2 tablespoons(small mound) shredded cheese in the center of each flattened biscuit. top with about 6 to 8 pepperoni slices, more if you wish. Keep filling away from edges.
  3. Tuck filling into center as you roll the biscuits beginning on one end to look like a hot dog roll.
  4. slice tops diagonally to allow venting, but only lightly, top layer of dough only.
  5. Bake at 350 F for about 15 to 17 minutes. Allow to cool for 3 to 4 minutes, be careful the melted cheese filling can get very hot.
